--- title: "Toggle Switch" layout: xtag root: "../" description: "A toggle switch allows the users to choose from two states and the changes are immediate." use_it: "When you have a binary setting." ---

Do's

  • - provide a label
  • - use it when you have a binary setting because it's cognitively less demanding than a checkbox

Dont's

  • - don't rename the on/off label unless you have extremely good reason. Even then, think twice.
  • - don't use it when the user probably know the exact target number. Use a text box instead
